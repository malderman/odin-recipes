Yum Yum Sauce

Description

Warning: this sauce is not quite a health food! HOWEVER, it’s a tasty way to amp up flavor and a little goes a very long way!

I wish I could share some deep secrets about how to make Yum Yum sauce. But, it’s so simple: throw the ingredients in a bowl and stir! Yes, it’s that easy.

Now, about the ingredients. It’s a mayonnaise-based sauce, with melted butter and tomato paste (or ketchup). We experimented with removing the butter in this one, but it tasted very strongly of mayo. Adding the butter helps to smooth out the sauce, so we would not suggest omitting it. Also, Yum Yum sauce typically calls for sugar, but we substituted it with maple syrup to keep it naturally sweet. Round it out with some garlic powder and paprika, and it’s done! Delicious and truly easy.

If you’d like to add a smoky flair, try using smoked paprika. It adds a complex undertone that’s a nice touch.

OK, now the question: how to serve it? Try with Crispy Cauliflower Tacos!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mayonnaise (vegan if desired)
  • 1 tablespoon melted butter (use coconut oil for vegan)
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon maple syrup
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 2 tablespoons water

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients together in a bowl.
  2. Store refrigerated for several months.
